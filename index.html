Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 24, 2024

Molave Labs ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Reasonix mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a safe, transparent, and enjoyable brain training experience.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information Stored Locally

To provide a personalized experience without compromising privacy, Reasonix stores the following data only on your device:

  • Your chosen username
  • Game scores, high scores, and statistics
  • Theme preferences (Light/Dark mode)
  • Daily challenge streak and completion status

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

1.2 Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and process certain data to serve relevant ads, as described in their policies:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device information and specifications
  • App usage data for ad personalization and analytics

You can read more about how Google manages data in Google's Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Local Data: We use your local data strictly to enable game functionality, save your progress, and maintain your settings.
  • Advertising Data: We use AdMob to display advertisements, which allows us to keep Reasonix free for everyone.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information. Data collection is limited to:

  • Google AdMob: For serving advertisements.
  • Legal Requirements: Only if strictly required by law.

4. Children's Privacy

Reasonix is designed for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. All gameplay data is stored locally, ensuring a safe environment for younger users in compliance with COPPA.

5. Your Rights & Choices

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete Data: Uninstalling the app instantly deletes all locally stored data.
  • Opt-out of Ads: You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings (Android Settings > Google > Ads > Delete advertising ID).
